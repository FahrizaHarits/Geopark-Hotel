Welcome To Our Hotel

Geopark Hotel is a hotel located in Sukabumi district which has a prominent geological wealth and natural beauty. This hotel is located near or within a geopark, which is an area recognized internationally for its unique geology. Geopark Hotel offers comfortable accommodation and complete facilities to its guests, while promoting nature conservation and education about the surrounding geological heritage. With its strategic location, Geopark Hotel provides an opportunity for visitors to explore the amazing geological wonders and natural beauty, while enjoying the modern facilities provided by the hotel.

Geopark Hotel is also committed to preserving the environment, implementing environmentally friendly practices, and supporting geological conservation efforts in the surrounding area. By combining the comforts of modern lodging with stunning natural beauty, Geopark Hotel is the ideal place for tourists who wish to experience an immersive geological adventure.
